The NYU
Mariachi

NYU Mariachi is a student-led musical ensemble at New York University dedicated to celebrating and sharing the rich traditions of Mexican mariachi music. Through performances on campus and across New York City, the group brings together students from diverse backgrounds to honor cultural heritage, build community, and showcase live mariachi through authentic instrumentation, musicianship, and spirit.
